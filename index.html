GET YOUR DOLLARS, GIRL

Interactive Storytelling Project

Welcome to Get Your Dollars, Girl - a 3-part interactive media project on labor conditions of drag artists in NYC. Due to power imbalances and a lack of structure in the NYC drag scene, conversations about the work conditions of nightlife performers aren't held often enough. The goal of Get Your Dollars, Girl is to raise public awareness of the struggles drag artists face, and spur conversations about how unfair such a booming industry can be towards those at its very core.
