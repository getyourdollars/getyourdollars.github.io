GET YOUR DOLLARS, GIRL

An analysis of labor and wages in NYC’s Queer Nightlife



Welcome to Get Your Dollars, Girl - an interactive storytelling project on labor conditions of drag artists in NYC.

Due to power imbalances and a lack of structure in the NYC drag scene, conversations about the labor conditions of nightlife

performers aren't held often enough. The goal of Get Your Dollars, Girl is to raise public awareness of the struggles drag

artists face, and spur conversations about how unfair such a booming industry can be towards those at its very foundation.
